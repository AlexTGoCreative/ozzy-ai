# OPSWAT AI Content Inspector Engine

OPSWAT AI Content Inspector requires separate licensing for MetaDefender to enable this module.

**OPSWAT AI Content Inspector** is an AI-driven content authenticity and document fraud detection engine for MetaDefender. It analyzes visual, textual, and structural signals to help organizations identify suspicious invoices, claim images, accident photos, and other high-risk content before review, approval, or payout. It also identifies whether digital content, including text and images, was created by a human or generated by an AI system.

The engine uses a hybrid detection methodology that combines metadata analysis, content fingerprinting, and multimodal AI classification to evaluate both content origin and document authenticity. By correlating signals across these layers, OPSWAT AI Content Inspector can distinguish AI-generated content from authentic material and surface fraud indicators in invoices, claim evidence, and identity documents. Detection results include confidence scores and explainability data, enabling administrators to understand the basis for each classification and fraud verdict.

## OPSWAT AI Content Inspector version and scope

Current version: v2.0 (general access, released 2026-06-10). The engine detects AI-generated content AND document fraud. Supported file types include JPG, PNG, WEBP, AVIF, BMP, PDF, TXT, MD.

## Key Capabilities

**Detect AI-generated text and images, and flag AI-generated document-fraud indicators**across commonly used file formats found in enterprise workflows such as claims intake, AP/invoice processing, and etc.**Analyze metadata patterns and content fingerprints**to identify synthetic generation, tampering, and forgery signals before content reaches downstream systems.**Enforce organizational content integrity and fraud-screening policies**based on detection outcomes , allow, flag, block, or route for human review at scale.
