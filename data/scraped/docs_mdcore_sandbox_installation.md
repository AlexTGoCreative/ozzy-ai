# Installation

The MetaDefender Aether (Adaptive Sandbox) Engine supports engine updates in either online or offline environments

## Engine updates

Go to **Settings > Module Update**

MetaDefender Core will automatically download all the Engines available in your license from our online update infrastructure, and download new Engine versions automatically based on your **Module Update** configuration.
